
# Simple
- Stim band (400gp)
	- When you heal, receive x1.5 HP
	- Increase the health gained to x2 HP efficiency
- Mind Stabilizer (600gp)
	- +1 to [[Efficiency]] saves per level.

# Complex
- Demon Blood Infuser (1,000gp)
	- +1 to Str and Con. Deal an additional 1d6+[[Efficiency|efc]] damage on melee attacks. As bonus enter *[[Demon Mode|Demon Mode]]* for rounds equal to your [[Efficiency]]. 
- Extra Arms  (2,500gp)
	- +1 to Str and Dex
	- Additional +1 to Str and Dex (500gp)
- Embedded Chest Laser Cannon (5,000gp)
	- As an action , activate the laser cannon. The laser shoots out in a 10' wide, 60' long beam. Any creature hit by the laser must make a DC 16 Dex saving throw, taking 8d12+[[Efficiency|efc]] damage, half as much on a success. After being fired, the cannon takes 1 minute to cool down.
	- Advanced Cooling System (1,000gp) reduce cooling time by 3 rounds per level. (max 2)
