# Abilities
- Sneak Attack (3pts)
	- When you have advantage on an attack, deal an additional 2d6 damage. Deal an additional 1d6 damage per level of sneak attack.
	- You gain sneak attack damage when the enemy you are attacking is within 5' of an ally. (6pts)
	- You can get sneak attack on a second attack. (6pts)

- Rage (2pts)
- Stat Increase (1pt)
- Leg Enhancements (1pt)
	- Increase movement speed by 10' per level.
	- Disengage as a bonus action.
- Extra Attack (3pts)
- Second Wind (2pts)
	- As a bonus action, heal 1D10+Con per level. You can use this ability up to the number of levels you have in this ability.
- Marksman
	- Gain +1 to ranged attacks, plus an additional +1 per level.
	- 
- Melee Master (1pt)
	- Gain +1 per level to melee attacks 
	- Attacks made with the off-hand get the modifier bonus to damage.
- Perseverance (1pt)
	- When you fail a saving throw, you may roll it again. You must use the new roll. You can use this ability once per long rest.
	- When you use this ability, you can reroll with advantage.
